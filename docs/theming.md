# 🎨 Theming

Personalize the look and feel of your Mission Control dashboard! The theming engine provides extensive customization options.

### Dark Mode Options

Choose how you want dark mode to behave:
-   🌙 **Manual Toggle:** Switch dark mode on or off manually via the theme picker.
-   🖥️ **Auto (OS Preference):** Mission Control can automatically follow your operating system's light or dark mode preference.
-   🌑 **Multiple Dark Gray Backgrounds:** Select from several shades of dark gray for the primary background when dark mode is active, allowing you to fine-tune the visual comfort.

### Accent Colors

Add a splash of color to your dashboard:
-   🌈 Choose from a palette of at least 20 distinct accent colors.
-   The selected accent color will be used for highlighting active elements, links, buttons, and other key UI components throughout the dashboard.
